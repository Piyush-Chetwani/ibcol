Frequently Asked Questions

How is your hackathon different than other hackathons?

We are not a hackathon. In fact, hackathons tend to produce as they advertise... hacks... aka not lasting solutions. While hackathons may have been popular and good for ideation for startups or when there is a very specific API or platform to build on, for a decentralised application (blockchain application), no way in hell a hackathon will produce anything useful. That’s why we have a rigorous and disciplined process for finding the most promising projects and teams.
