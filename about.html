About The International Blockchain Olympiad

Our Vision / Mission

The International Blockchain Olympiad is an annual competition for students and professionals to present their solutions for various categories within the blockchain industry.

Competitors can choose to compete in 10 categories, each will represent various parts of the blockchain industry. Competitors are not limited to the amount of categories they can join. Each category will have it's own set of prizes and judges.
